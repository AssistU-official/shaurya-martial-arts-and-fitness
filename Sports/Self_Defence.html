Self Defence

Empower yourself with techniques that prioritize safety, awareness, and confidence in any situation.

What is Self Defence?

Self-defence is the art of protecting oneself from potential threats or attacks. It is not only about fighting back but also about awareness, prevention, and smart decision-making.

It equips individuals with practical skills to stay safe in everyday life, whether at home, at work, or in public spaces. From children to adults, self-defence is a life skill for everyone.

Benefits of Self Defence

Safety & Awareness

Helps you recognize threats early and react effectively.

Confidence

Builds self-esteem and the ability to stay calm under pressure.

Practical Skills

Learn easy-to-use techniques suitable for real-life situations.

Stages of Learning & Effort Required

Beginner

1–3 months: Awareness training, basic blocks, stance & movement.

Intermediate

6–12 months: Counter techniques, situational defence & control drills.

Advanced

1–3 years: Multiple attacker defence, weapon disarming & sparring.

Expert

3+ years: Mastery, teaching others, refining awareness & discipline.
