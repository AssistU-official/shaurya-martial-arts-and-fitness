About Us

Shaurya Martial Arts & Self Defence — dedicated to empower everyone, building strength in body, clarity in mind, and courage in spirit.

Our Story

Shaurya Martial Arts & Self Defence is instrumental in learning martial arts for all age groups and genders. Especially to learn self defense, which is one of the key elements of martial arts. With a team of qualified trainers trained both in India and abroad, and a well-equipped academy, we provide training with a different approach.

Our flexible training methods and rigorous safety measures allow us to teach martial arts even to those people who don’t have previous experience or those apprehensive about physical exercise. We believe martial arts is not just training the body, but shaping the mind and uplifting the spirit.

Our philosophy: Martial Arts is a life art — not exclusive to the physically strong. It develops fitness, endurance, flexibility, and confidence in every individual. It teaches discipline, respect, resilience, and inner strength.

Founder

Meet the Founder

Piyush Waghade – Chief Coach

Piyush Waghade is the visionary behind Shaurya Martial Arts. With over two decades of martial arts expertise, he has combined passion, discipline, and leadership to build a one-of-a-kind academy.

A Black Belt in Taekwondo, certified in Krav Maga (Singapore), and deeply experienced in Kickboxing and Boxing, Piyush brings world-class training into every session.

His philosophy is simple: martial arts is more than combat — it’s about resilience, focus, and respect. He ensures Shaurya Martial Arts remains not just a training ground, but a community of growth and empowerment.

Philosophy & Values

Discipline & Respect

True mastery comes from discipline — in training, mindset, and behavior. Respect for ourselves, others, and the art is integral to all we do.

Safety & Growth

Safety is our top priority. Every technique and class nurtures growth while keeping students secure and confident.

Mind over Muscle

While physical strength matters, mental clarity and emotional control sustain progress. We train both body and mind.

Community & Belonging

We aim to build a community where everyone feels included, supported, and empowered through their martial arts journey.
